Vegetarian Festival

Vegetarian festival or in some places called the tradition of " Thu Sil Kin Phak " (Keeping the Precepts, Eat Vegetables). The lunar day is set from the first day of the first moon to the ninth day of the ninth month according to the Chinese calendar every year. Total 9 days.

Monkey Festival

Monkey Festival is an important traditional festival held every year in Lopburi Province. First started since 2532 and will be held every last Sunday of November at Phra Kan Shrine and Phra Prang Sam Yot for monkeys to eat officially once a year after humans came to build the city until the locals as monkeys must become only residents.

Loy Krathong Festival

Loy Krathong tradition has been passed down for a long time. This custom was established as a means of salvation and apology to the goddess Ganga. For Thailand, scheduled to be held in all areas throughout the country. It falls on the 15th day of the 12th lunar month according to the Thai lunar calendar.

Songkran Festival

Songkran ceremony was a ritual that took place within the family main element of the ceremony to counter the meaning of summer. The moment when the sun moves into Aries. On this day, water will be sprinkled on each other for freshness. There are blessings from adults.
